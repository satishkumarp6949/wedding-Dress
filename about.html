Our Story

Lumina Bridal Couture was born from a passion for timeless beauty and artisanal excellence. Every gown is meticulously handcrafted in our Indian atelier, blending traditional techniques with modern silhouettes to create one-of-a-kind bridal masterpieces.

From the first sketch to the final stitch, we pour our hearts into creating gowns that reflect your individuality, elegance, and the romance of your special day.
